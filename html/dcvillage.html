DC Village

The DC Village concept can be combined with solar panels and overpass TDWGs in a clean energy suite that enables 5 separate sources of renewable energy to recharge the Hydrogen fuel cell and plug-in vehicles of the future. The cumulative output of all will help to compensate for the intermittent nature of each.

DC Village Cities

DC Villages can be sited deep within cities, allowing easy and quick access to pedestrians who wish to get exercise and get paid for it. Each device in the park will allow for a different set of muscles to be exercised, and enforce a short rest period between events.

DC Village Outdoors

DC Village Outdoors Parks enable parents to exercise while they keep close watch over their children. An audible signal sounding 30 minutes after sign-in will let each User know that his or her time limit is up; this will prevent overexertion as well as open up space for the next person. After this warning sound, the User will not be compensated for any energy produced on that machine, but can go to another of the HEH devices after a short rest.
