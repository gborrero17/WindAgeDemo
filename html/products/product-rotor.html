WindAge TDWG Systems Mountings

A WindAge TDWG Rotor Assembly is light in weight, at less than 400 pounds, and securely mounts to its own concrete or steel I-beam under highway overpasses through either drilling and bolting or welding. Each is designed with adjustable mounting arms for easily-adapted installations over angled traffic lanes and various mounting elevations. These arms are locked in a position that always allows at least 6" as a safety buffer over the legal maximum truck trailer height of about 13' 6". This adjustability of the mounting arms is also employed when the units are attached to dedicated mounting structures as commonly used to attach Interstate informational signs.
