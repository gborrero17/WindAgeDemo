Emissions-Free Plastic Repurposing Plant

Third World countries have large problems with collecting and reusing plastic trash because they often do not have government-funded trash collectors, or landfills. The Emissions-Free Plastic Repurposing Plant herein described is specifically designed to be shipped to Third World countries in standard containers, to be set up quickly, and operate with no smoke, fumes, or sound emissions.
